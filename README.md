GearTrack is a robust internal management system designed for companies to streamline the lifecycle of their equipment. From simple tracking of assets to complex trip planning and client assignments, GearTrack ensures accountability and operational efficiency.

ğŸŒŸ Key Features
 Equipment Tracking: Always know who has what. Monitor the status (Available, In-Use, Maintenance) and the current holder of every asset.

Trip Management (Bundling): Users can create "Trips" to reserve multiple pieces of equipment simultaneously for a specific project or mission.

Collaborative Booking: Need a partner? Users can book a colleague to accompany them on a trip.

Automated Email Notifications: When a team member is booked for a trip, the system automatically triggers a detailed notification email to their inbox.

Admin Assignment: Administrators can assign specific employees to manage particular clients, ensuring clear lines of communication and responsibility.

Interactive Dashboard: A centralized view for admins to monitor equipment distribution and team workloads.

ğŸ—ï¸ Core Workflow
Inventory: Admins populate the system with company equipment and client lists.

Request & Trip: An employee selects necessary gear or creates a "Trip" for a multi-asset mission.

Collaboration: The employee invites a teammate. The teammate receives an automated email with the schedule and gear list.

Assignment: The Admin assigns the employee to a Client, linking the equipment usage to a specific business account.

Return: Once the task is complete, gear is checked back in, updating the status to "Available."
